\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}API-compatibility wrapper for Python's OpenSSL wrapped socket object.

Note: _makefile_refs, _drop(), and _reuse() are needed for the garbage
collector of PyPy.
\end{DoxyVerb}
